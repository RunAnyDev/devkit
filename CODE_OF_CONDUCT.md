# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want DevKit to be welcoming, respectful, and useful for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarity.
- Keep discussions focused on project quality and user value.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or repeated off-topic comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation for behavior that harms the project community.
